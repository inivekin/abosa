A Ballad o Sundered Aengus

tap or hover to latinize


Vo tae void go? I dinnae ken.
Am lost afloat sea asimmer
so devoid o copernican
sentiments it sets me center.
Crystalline vines wi latticed lines,
along briefest reef follow feet,
refracting shines in eyes o mine,
buckers an birls me tae stane seat.
And staring at both ailing suns,
middae, dreich blau - loathsome - dulled stars
owre ma avail spectrums,
cannae spy auld homesun pulsars.
Nor horned serpent o gallilae
that streekt frae nau hollowed an cracked
ooter airm six twin nebulae,
mae it lang cradled, nau gane facked.
Then low orbit imitations,
stellations o tetrahedrands,
aglow raw grit scintillations,
well oceans oot frae tear glands
as if my ship that dust the skies,
that gave grief on way an tae leave,
reflect as real in my eyes -
nau lost escape winnae reprieve.
Hither struggled an struggle still,
heid weighed down by gravitation,
bothered none an shrugged by winds shrill,
tugging kite wi restless motion.
Ceaseless noises o lowlands loch
host also EM tidal crest
o drowning cries that act tae block
mae call o distress: "jus tae rest..."


Nae rest there was, I would nae keep
oot there because exosuit leaks
cool air tae heat, wi quick'ning beep -
those caught who find exit first seeks.
So in steam, clouding panoptic,
toeing streams I'd nae dare wade through,
trusting in fields magnetic,
puir rusting shields hae paid true.
Is nervous thing tae be you see,
metal-wrapped, unacclimated -
oot o reach o friends, family -
little chap love-lacked an jaded
driving oneself through endless void
finding themself in blinding fogs
alive wi self, naut paranoid
where only self will haunt the bogs.
Deterministic anxious waves
o bugs in ears an fungoids grown,
are all thats left in coward caves,
I've chosen tae suffer the known.
...til worry o higher degrees
start wearing awa fissured plates
swapping odds o bayesian lieklies
an driving me tae unknown fates.
Not tae be mistaken fae brave,
ma sprint tae screaming frequencies
'twere more a run frae painful grave
o lustful molten rock an seas.
When gustful chaos quiets some
approaching shores o placid dunes,
I ken the cause when that plight come,
the deserts sinking, flacid croons.


I'm sick o this I just wan home,
nae tricks o licht nor migraine chrome,
but licks o flame an green biome,
thick brick o page-filled, langsyne tome.
Egad! Alas, quaking sands sift,
an heids, like mine, heavy, ebb fast.
Sea clad o glass form a grand rift
tae skyline periph'ry spread vast.
At edge where glass grains fall both ways,
atop a point langrangian,
am safe as Last Man o blank bays,
but subject tae blare gaussian.
Those sisyphean aft ashame,
they'd dream tae take this battle slow,
frenetic static hits me same
yet makes ma thin bones rattle so.
Bevy o antennas wired,
rhizomes strafing past afloating,
adjoint tae roaming ore spired
via hanging mast it's toting.
Mired above the gaping black,
slowly enough so I can track,
does it drag hole or hole drag back?
What are the guards agin a hack?
Peering in pit, dark an unlit,
Swallowing grit tae mantle crust.
Hid frae suns hit, nature unwrit,
Intent undescrit yet I still trust.
Hypnotised mute, straying astute,
Right at the root there's upward thrust.
Loose parachute, heat more acute,
Will root bear fruit? I know it must.


Weight o planets always humbling,
left feckless thoughts unconcious hid,
like up-turned insect flights fumbling,
like reckless, concientious kid,
falling again as fools tend to,
when leaping aff well-meaning hicht,
dinnae regard the hopes untrue,
the real tops intent in micht.
I hide awa wi shame I learnt,
behind replay o muscle logs,
an accept humility earnt
as gear caucht in cosmic cogs.
In cybernetics take over,
under mechanical descent,
nae help tae ken parabola,
the lack o choice disnae relent.
Mind snaps back wi taut o lines caucht,
a rushed retrace o path unsure
finds gap black was naut o kinds thocht,
but gushing fount frae matter lure.
Lo, a method moulding spacetime.
Woe, besotted wi inertia,
robbing me my grace I climb,
stabbing kite like pest olfersia.
Vofur pull frae banks o data
genus words ad ataxia?
Kenning taxonomic strata
disna aid ataraxia.
I grasp frae hook-edged wand-knife held
machinic drive isna enough
tae clasp forsook, dredged land-waif felled
frae cynic dive tae inert slough.


Let the lack o cause by nature
indicate a fleeting reason,
take up slack an rouse tae future,
even if there's weeds an fleas in.
Translate stacking, branching pathways
o the many cornered maze tree,
once considered lazy delays,
same repeating sierpinski.
Instead note O so simple truth,
time itself isnae time perceievd,
deadends I hit when in my youth
nau linger longer than believed.
Looking down on route unwinding,
how I searched for centroid secret,
I'd nae get there without finding,
in corners, notetakers scrawled writ:
"Atoms, once scattered, will again.
These photo-electric clockworks
quantized to matter still refrain
through echo-concentric networks.
No matter if you stand near,
or scatter as you're nae more seen,
you were always tae be here,
here you will always hae been."
And so I know why still I ficht,
because they faucht fae me before,
they've quietened tae null-like nicht
but grief fae them breaks walls an floor.
Hidden pervasive cables strobing,
interconnected, resonate.
I flood systems overloading
wi chants o lives concatenate.


Constant loudness seemed so massive,
rampant shrewdness o racous breaths,
sets like sun nau, less oppressive,
lurking down in vacuous depths.
I'd tried hard wi kernel o code,
in each part you were embedded,
may not last in fractal download,
but at least am clear-heided.
Shifting forces makes sand syrup,
moving land tae stable new state.
Though beacon is nae more corrupt,
Am caught in sands an nau must wait.
Din encompassing silent gane,
my bones can rest under my skin.
Nae more enduring violent pain,
Nae poke nor stab, nae prod nor pin.
These moments some think macabre,
it slips my mind that you did go,
but remnant binds simulacra,
like sticker stars on ceilings glow.
In pretense, if you had nae gane,
I play that you lay at surface,
suspending thochts tae keep me sane,
that clay animate serves purpose.
Longer was I not an won't be,
than ever do I patient wait,
but pulsar clocks time not mem'ry
am holding through the hours late.
I'll leave my haun above an free,
tae grow like seeds take time tae wake,
an know I can be company,
if someone needs a haun tae take.
