A Ballad of Sundered Aengus


Vo tae void go? I dinnae ken.
Am lost afloat sea asimmer
so devoid o copernican
sentiments it sets me center.
Crystalline vines wi latticed lines,
along briefest reef follow feet,
Refracting shines in eyes o mine,
buckers and birls me tae stane seat.
And staring at both ailing suns,
Middae, dreich blau - loathsome - dulled stars
owre ma avail spectrums,
cannae spy auld homesun pulsars.
Nor horned serpent o gallilae
that streekt frae nau hollowed an cracked
ooter airm six twin nebulae,
mae it lang cradled, nau gane facked.
Then low orbit imitations,
stellations o tetrahedrands,
aglow raw grit scintillations,
well oceans oot frae tear glands
as if my ship that dust the skies,
that gave grief on way and to leave,
reflect as real in my eyes -
nau lost escape winnae reprieve.
Hither struggled and struggle still,
heid weighed down by gravitation,
bothered none and shrugged by winds shrill,
tugging kite wi restless motion.
Unceasing noise o lowlands loch
host also EM tidal crest
o drowning cries that act tae block
mae call o distress: "jus tae rest..."


Nae rest there was, I would nae keep
Oot there because exosuit leaks
cool air tae heat, wi quick'ning beep -
those caught who find exit first seeks.
So in steam, clouding panoptic,
toeing streams I'd nae dare wade through,
trusting in fields magnetic,
puir rusting shields hae paid true.
Is nervous thing tae be you see,
metal-wrapped, unacclimated -
Oot o reach o friends, family -
little chap love-lacked and jaded
driving self through an endless void
and finds themself in blinding fogs
alive wi self, naut paranoid
where only self will haunt the bogs.
Deterministic anxious waves
o bugs in ears an fungoids grown,
are all thats left in coward caves,
I've chosen tae suffer the known.
...til worry o higher degrees
start wearing awa fissured plates
swapping odds o bayesian lieklies
an driving me tae unknown fates.
Not tae be mistaken fae brave,
ma sprint tae screaming frequencies
twere more a run frae painful grave
o lustful molten rock an seas.
When gustful chaos quiets some
approaching shores o placid dunes,
I ken the cause when that plight come,
the deserts sinking, flacid croons.


I'm sick of this I just wan home,
Nae tricks of licht nor migraine chrome,
but licks o flame an green biome,
Thick brick of page-filled, langsyne tome,
Egad! Alas, quaking sands sift,
an heids heavy as mine ebb fast.
Sea clad o glass form a grand rift
tae periph'ry skylines spread vast.
At edge where glass grains fall both ways,
atop a point langrangian,
am safe as Last Man o blank bays,
but subject tae blare gaussian.
Those sisyphean aft ashame,
They'd dream tae take this battle slow,
frenetic static hits me same
yet makes ma thin bones rattle so.
Bevy o antennas wired,
rhizomes strafing past afloating,
adjoint tae roaming ore spired,
via hanging mast it's toting.
Mired above the gaping black,
Slow enough so I can keep track,
Does it drag hole or hole drag back?
What are the guards agin a hack?
Peering in pit, dark an unlit,
Swallowing grit tae mantle crust.
Hid frae suns hit, nature unwrit,
Intent undescrit yet I still trust.
Hypnotised mute, straying astute,
Right at the root there's upward thrust.
Loose parachute, heat more acute,
Will root bear fruit? I know it must.
